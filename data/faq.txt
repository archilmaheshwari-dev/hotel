Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out
can be requested and are subject to availability.

The hotel offers free WiFi throughout the property, a fitness center open 24/7, and a
swimming pool open from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

Breakfast is included with all bookings and is served in the main restaurant from 7:00 AM
to 10:30 AM.

Cancellations made more than 48 hours before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations
within 48 hours of check-in are non-refundable.

Pets are allowed in select rooms with a one-time cleaning fee of $30. Please mention this
when booking so we can assign an appropriate room.

Parking is available on-site and is complimentary for all guests.

The hotel is located 15 minutes from the airport and offers a paid shuttle service on
request.
